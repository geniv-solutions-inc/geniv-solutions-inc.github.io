About

Our founder, George Davis, is a man of change. Throughout his illustrious career he has driven change for the good and has created a positive impact in the world.

His career includes being a software test engineer, software developer, platform engineer, AWS DevOps and Cloud specialist and Solutions Architect at some of the global organizations in Canada, US and the UK.

GenIV Solutions Inc. is the next step in the journey to bring a positive impact not only to the local community in Kelowna, BC, but also across Canada and the United States.

We look forward to serving you with their IT needs.
