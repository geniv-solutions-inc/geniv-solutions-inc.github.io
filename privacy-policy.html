Gen IV Solutions Inc. ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data you entrust to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

1. Information We Collect

We collect only essential information required to provide and support our SaaS services:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Company name (if applicable)
  • User credentials (e.g., login info)
  • Technical data (e.g., IP address, browser type, usage logs)

We do not collect sensitive categories of data (e.g., biometric, health, or financial data) unless explicitly required and consented to.

2. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on the following lawful grounds:

  • Performance of a contract: To provide you with access to our platform and services
  • Consent: When you voluntarily provide data (e.g., email signup)
  • Legal obligation: To comply with laws or regulatory requirements
  • Legitimate interest: To improve security, user experience, and service quality

3. Use of Personal Data

Your data is used strictly to:

  • Provide and manage your account
  • Deliver our core services
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve platform functionality and security
  • Comply with applicable laws

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may share data only:

  • With trusted service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, authentication services) under data processing agreements
  • To comply with legal or regulatory obligations
  • In connection with a merger or acquisition, with adequate notice and safeguards

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined above or to comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption
  • Access controls
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure data centers

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request data deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Under CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we collect and why
  • Request access to and deletion of your data
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Exercise these rights without discrimination

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [Insert Contact Email].

8. International Transfers

If you are located outside the country where our servers are hosted, your data may be transferred internationally. We use standard contractual clauses and other safeguards to ensure compliance with data protection laws.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you through our platform or by email, if applicable.
